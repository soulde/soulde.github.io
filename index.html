🙋🏻‍♂️ Introduction

Hi, I'm Colin Lee (李雨璟). I am studying for a master's degree in control science and Engineering at NJUST. I have been deeply involved in the Robomaster, which has fueled my immense interest in robotics. At present, I am also co-running a company with former teammates, focusing on the design of robotics modules. Furthermore, my dual-major undergraduate education has significantly influenced me, expanding my intellectual horizons and allowing me to delve into the knowledge of multiple disciplines. This diverse background has given me a broad understanding of robotics, while also nurturing a strong curiosity for new knowledge. I am eager to continue my journey in robotics and explore the latest advancements in this field.

My research interests include Multi-Agent System, Reinforcement Learning and Robotics. I have successfully undertaken projects in software design, embedded systems, and robot navigation. At present, I am engaged in projects concerning embodied intelligence, and I am researching the application of deep reinforcement learning within distributed robotic systems.
